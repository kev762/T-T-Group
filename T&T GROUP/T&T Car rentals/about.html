About T&T Car Rentals

T&T Car Rentals is Rwanda's trusted partner for premium, executive, and group transportation. We offer modern, maintained vehicles and professional drivers so every journey is effortless and memorable.

Purpose-driven

Mission & Vision

Everything we do is centered on dependable service, premium experiences, and Rwanda-wide coverage.

Our Mission

Provide stress-free mobility for executives, families, and groups by combining a modern fleet, professional chauffeurs, and customer-first service that is available whenever our clients need to move.

  • Rapid confirmations for every booking
  • Meticulously detailed vehicles
  • Professional, multilingual chauffeurs

Our Vision

Become Rwanda’s most trusted mobility partner, recognized for delivering premium experiences, rapid response, and nationwide coverage for business, tourism, and diplomatic travel.

  • Executive-class hospitality standards
  • Digital-first experience from inquiry to drop-off
